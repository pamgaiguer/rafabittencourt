Show all the Your Facebook Album & Photos at your Blog Without INSTALL any FACEBOOK APP.

== Description ==

This Plugin Allow to show all the your Facebook Album & Photos at your blog. when you click on any Ablum its shows the photos, for this you have to only put your Facebook Account ID. The Picture Gallery Shows with Lightbox jQuery effects, when you click on the Album Photos.

<h4>More Information</h4>

Visit the <a href="http://www.gcodelabs.com/wp-plugin-facebook-photos.php" title="gcodelabs plugin support">gCodeLabs Website</a> for more information about Facebook Album & Photos Plugin.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `facebook-photos` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Enter your Facebook Acount ID by clicking the Facebook Photos link under Settings menu.
4. For the Facebook account Id , go to the facebook photo section and see the ID in the URL.
5. Enjoy it !!!
